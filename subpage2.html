About Teen Wolf

Teen Wolf is an action-packed supernatural drama that follows Scott McCall, a teenager who discovers he has been turned into a werewolf. As he navigates his new identity, Scott must balance his high school life with the dangers of the supernatural world.
Filled with suspense, friendship, and heart-pounding moments, Teen Wolf explores themes of loyalty, courage, and self-discovery. The series became a fan favorite, known for its intense storylines and compelling characters.
